What do we do?

At Home Security limited, we do our best to help our customers feel safer. Our belief is that a safe and secure home, is a truly happy home

How?

Our products are top of the line and have many features. Our cameras use face recognition, which can recognise when anything resembling a face is shown to the camera. We also have a built-in face identification system which allows users to add a database of images of people they know, so that the camera will identify them. Through our website, customers can, using their unique account, view data the camera stores. For example, if your friend John was at the door at 12 o clock today, that would be stored in the website, or if an unknown person passed the camera at a different time, that would also be stored.

Why do we do this?

We believe in today's world, it is important to have access to a home security feed wherever you are. Whether you are at work or somewhere else. Many companies provide doorbells that do just this, however we decided to go a different route. One issue we saw was that none of these devices provide access to any data, or let you view your data through any web browser, rather than an app.
